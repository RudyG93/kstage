### ✧･ﾟ: Intro To Live Show Updates! :･ﾟ✧

_**Hello everyone and welcome!!**_

 Just a couple of things so everyone knows how everything works!**1)** There is only one person running this account, so please be mindful of that. Anytime you see the potted plant (🪴), that’s just me talking about something besides line ups.**2)** I will try and post the line ups on the day of the show or the night before.**3)** If you want to use my line ups for yourself **please ask!** My DMs are open! Or just credit me in your post.**4)** I am **_NOT_** affiliated with any of the shows. I get the line ups from the official sources. I’ve linked them [here](https://liveshowupdatess.carrd.co/#showinfo)───── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ─────_**When do the line ups go up?**_

 I will post the line up on Carrd the night before the show (for Music Core & Inkigayo it will be two (2) or three (3) nights before) and then the final version of the line up will go up on social media the day of the show.───── ⋆⋅☆⋅⋆ ─────

**Notes:**

 I might not be able to post updates for Show Champion, I'll still put the lineup up on here (Carrd). And for the weekend shows (Music Core & Inkigayo) there might be some weekends where I won't be able to do updates during the shows.Thank you for understanding! 💚
